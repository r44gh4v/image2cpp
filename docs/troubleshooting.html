Troubleshooting

Output is mostly blank

Lower threshold and recheck image contrast. Monochrome conversion can remove faint details at high thresholds.

Bitmap appears inverted

Toggle invert/invert background controls and verify your display color polarity.

Image looks stretched

Use Fit (preserve ratio) scaling unless your target display explicitly needs stretched output.

Firmware too large

Reduce dimensions and frame count; prefer static images over large GIF sequences for constrained boards.
